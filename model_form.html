Model Release
Form

Last updated: 15th March 2021
We Should Create create commercial images that are used to promote our Customers products or services. A Model Release Form is an agreement between We Should Create (and it's Customers) and you, the model, for commercial use of any images that are shot at a photoshoot you take part in. The stipulations in a model release form outline what we can and cannot do with the images we shoot whilst you are modelling. Model Release Forms offer protection to We Should Create, our Customers, and to you as the model.

Model Release Form

In consideration of my engagement as a model, and for other good and valuable consideration herein acknowledged as received, I hereby acknowledge the following rights and permissions belong to We Should Create ("Photographer"), legal representatives, and assigns, those for whom the Photographer is acting, and those acting with their authority and permission.

They have the irrevocable, perpetual and unrestricted right and permission to take, use, re-use, sell, publish, and republish photographic portraits or pictures of me or in which I may be included, in whole or in part, or composite or distorted in character or form, without restriction as to changes or alterations, in conjunction with my own or a fictitious name, or reproductions thereof in colour or otherwise, made through any medium at their studio or elsewhere, and in any and all media now or hereafter known, specifically including but not limited to print media and distribution over the internet for illustration, promotion, art, editorial, advertising, trade, or any other purpose whatsoever.

I specifically consent to the digital compositing or distortion of the portraits or pictures, including without restriction any changes or alterations as to colour, size, shape, perspective, context, foreground or background. I also consent to the use of any published matter in conjunction with such photographs. I hereby waive any right that I may have to inspect or approve the finished product or products and the advertising copy or other matter that may be used in connection with them or the use to which they may be applied.

I hereby release, discharge, and agree to hold harmless the Photographer, legal representatives, and assigns, and all persons acting under their permission or authority or those for whom they are acting, from any liability by virtue of any blurring, distortion, alteration, optical illusion, or use in composite form, whether intentional or otherwise, that may occur or be produced in the taking of such photographs or in any subsequent processing of them, as well as any publication of them, including without limitation any claims for libel or violation of any right of publicity or privacy.

I hereby warrant that I am of full age and have the right to contract in my own name. I have read the above authorisation, release, and agreement, prior to its execution, and I am fully familiar with the contents of this form. This form shall be binding upon me and my heirs, legal representatives, and assigns.

I have read and agree to this model release form and the Privacy Policy
